📝 Formal Attire Guide

Formal Shirt

Solid colors like white, light blue, or cream. Long sleeves preferred.

Blazer/Jacket

Navy, black, or charcoal. Should fit well at shoulders.

Tie (Optional)

Solid or simple pattern. Should reach belt buckle.

Formal Pants

Dark colors, well-pressed. No jeans or cargo pants.

Formal Shoes

Leather, closed-toe. Black or brown. Clean and polished.

✅ Do's

  • • Iron your clothes
  • • Keep hair neat
  • • Minimal accessories
  • • Neutral colors

❌ Don'ts

  • • Bright/loud colors
  • • Casual wear
  • • Heavy perfume
  • • Flashy jewelry
